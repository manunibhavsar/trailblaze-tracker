The Story Behind the Tracker
ABOUT THIS PROJECT
Inspired by the Cosmos · Built for the Journey
The Inspiration

The first time I saw the Astral Express cutting through the stars — this sleek, gothic locomotive hurtling through open space — something just clicked. It wasn't just a ship. It was a vibe. A mood. A whole philosophy packed into one stunning piece of art.

Honkai: Star Rail's visual identity is unlike anything else. It merges the grandeur of space opera with the warmth of a train journey — the idea that adventure is the destination, not just a path to it. That blend of deep blues, celestial golds, and cosmic purples became the only possible choice for a personal productivity tracker.

If I'm going to stare at a dashboard every day, it might as well feel like I'm riding the Astral Express.

Why Honkai: Star Rail
The Art Direction
Every frame of HSR looks like a painting. The character designs, the environments, the UI — it's all crafted with extraordinary care. That level of visual polish is what I wanted to bring into my daily workflow.
The Cosmic Scale
Hopping between planets and star systems on a literal space train is the most ambitious setting in any gacha game. It made the idea of "tracking your journey" feel genuinely epic — not just a to-do list.
The Astral Express
The ship itself is the heart of it all. A locomotive in space — old-world elegance meets interstellar adventure. That contradiction is beautiful, and it's exactly the energy I want when I sit down to be productive.
The Trailblaze Spirit
The game is literally about blazing your own trail across the universe. As a personal metaphor for growth, discipline, and daily progress — it doesn't get more fitting than that.
"The stars don't care about your destination — they only know the light you leave behind as you pass."
